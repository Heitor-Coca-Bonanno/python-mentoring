# Example (filled in) — Making a Cup of Tea

## Title
Make a cup of tea

## Inputs
- mug
- teabag
- kettle + water
- optional: sugar, milk

## Steps (numbered)
1. Fill the kettle with water.
2. Turn the kettle on.
3. Put the teabag in the mug.
4. Wait until the water is boiling.
5. Pour hot water into the mug.
6. Wait 3 minutes.
7. Remove the teabag.

## Decisions (if / else)
- **IF** you want sugar:
  - add 1 teaspoon of sugar and stir
- **ELSE**:
  - skip sugar

## Loop (repeat / until)
- **REPEAT**:
  - stir the tea
- **UNTIL** the sugar is dissolved

## Output
A mug of tea ready to drink.
